About us

EASTMONT ELECTRICALS LTD was licensed by the Energy and Petroleum Regulatory Authority (EPRA) as a Class B electrical contractor and has been in operation since 2010. We provide installation, maintenance, and repair of electrical systems for commercial, industrial, institutional and domestic projects.

Over the years, we have built a reputation as a trusted, reliable, and dependable power solutions partner, delivering safe and efficient services to our clients.

Services

We provide a wide range of professional electrical solutions tailored for industrial, commercial, institutional, and domestic projects.

  • Electrical repair & service of industrial, institutional, and domestic installations
  • Underground cabling and termination
  • Motor and pump installation and servicing
  • Transformer installation
  • CCTV installations
  • Refrigeration and air conditioning
  • Generator installation and servicing
  • Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) installation and maintenance
  • Electrical project management
  • Electrical supplies
